¡Bienvenidos!

At Marbella Restaurant lunch and dinner are always a memorable experience. Relax in an elegant environment and enjoy the distinctive cuisine as prepared by our Chef , who is proud to present the most flavorful dishes from various regions of Spain as well as a number of French and Italian masterpieces. Our Chef's ambition is to offer each client a memorable dining experience. Memorable for the indisputable quality of the ingredients, expert preparation, and beautiful presentation.

Authentic Spanish Dishes

For starters, try our Sopa de Ajo and savor the pleasing aroma of this delicious Garlic soup, or sample our rich Caldo Gallego- a Galician soup with beef, pork, and vegetables. Also, discover the goodness of traditional dishes with fresh fish and seafood, as well as chicken and beef. In the mood for Paella? This tempting dish originated in the Valencia region of Spain, but our chef has elevated Paella to an artform. Try our Paella ala Valenciana with lobster, seafood, chicken, rice, and sausage--Unforgetable!!
